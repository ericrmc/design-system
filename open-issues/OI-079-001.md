## The discrepancy

`state/migrations/001-initial.sql` declares **17 tables**: `agent_runs, commitments, decision_alternatives, decisions, deliberations, engine_feedback, objects, perspectives, read_log, refs, role_write_capabilities, schema_migrations, sessions, spec_versions, subtraction_log, synthesis_points, work_items`.

078 D-10 set the table budget at **≤ 16 tables**, "breachable with cause." D-10's enumeration listed: sessions, objects, decisions, decision_alternatives, spec_versions, perspectives, deliberations, refs, commitments, engine_feedback, work_items, role_write_capabilities, read_log, subtraction_log, schema_migrations, agent_runs — **16 tables, including `objects`, omitting `synthesis_points`**.

`specifications/engine-manifest.md` enumerated **15 tables in prose**, omitting both `objects` (which D-10 included) and `synthesis_points` (the legitimate calibrated breach).

So the schema is +1 over D-10 (synthesis_points), and the manifest is −2 versus the schema (omits both objects and synthesis_points). The two arithmetic gaps have different causes: the +1 is a calibrated structural addition (T-14 has no other home); the −2 is a manifest prose omission unrelated to the budget.

## Resolution

S081 D-1 (substrate `D-S002-1`) ratifies 17 as the engine-v17 table count via the existing "breachable with cause" clause in 078 D-10. The cause record is narrow: `synthesis_points` carries T-14 (convergence requires ≥2 source_perspectives, enforced as a CHECK constraint) and has no other structural home that does not re-introduce the prose-state failure mode. The ratification is **not** a general relaxation of the table budget; future additions are evaluated against D-10's 16 + cause individually.

S081 D-1 also updates `specifications/engine-manifest.md`'s substrate enumeration from 15 names to 17, listing both `objects` (already in D-10) and `synthesis_points` (the calibrated breach), with annotation calling out that `objects` is the citable_alias indirection table powering T-01.

The minority position from S081's deliberation (P-2-adversarial, anthropic-family) — that the table-count budget is itself a derived quantity that should be replaced rather than ratified, because the breach mechanism plus manifest prose drift produced two miscounts of two different tables across three sessions — is **not** acted on under the 078 D-5 release gate (such a change would substantively rewrite D-10), but is preserved as **OI-081-001** for post-gate reconsideration.
