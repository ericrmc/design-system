# Laurel Delta assumption ledger — v1

The ledger catalogues what the session is taking as **given**,
**constraint**, **assumption**, or **decision**, with per-row audit
metadata so Session 002 can trace any piece of reasoning back to its
provenance.

**Distinctions (per `provenance/001-session/02-decisions.md` D-002):**

- **`constraint` (CON-*)** — something the session cannot vary within
  its remit. Challenged only by exceeding the application's bounds.
- **`given-flagged-for-survey` (GIV-*)** — something the brief states
  as fact that the session has *surveyed and accepted* as input per
  the anti-laundering rule. Visible, not silent.
- **`assumption` (ASM-*)** — something the session must commit to in
  order to proceed, which is not stated by the brief and is not
  forced by a constraint. Assumptions carry falsification conditions.
- **`decision` (DEC-*)** — a choice made this session between
  surveyed alternatives, with rejected options preserved in
  `provenance/001-session/02-decisions.md`. Decisions are revisable
  by future sessions.

**Cross-reference conventions:**

- `model_refs` points to `system-model.md` IDs.
- `source` column values: `brief:§X` = brief section; `session-
  chosen` = adopted in Session 001 deliberation; `inferred-from` =
  inferred from brief but not brief-stated; `external-surveyed` =
  pretrained-distribution frame surfaced and declined.
