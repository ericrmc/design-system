## Q1. Which §8 remediation shape is right for MAD at 6,403 words?

**Position:** A.2 split. Create `multi-agent-deliberation-oi004.md` holding the four OI-004-scoped sections (Closure Criteria for OI-004, Criterion-4 Articulation for OI-004, Acceptable Participant Kinds for OI-004, Closure Procedure for OI-004 — 1,463 words per brief §3.1). **Reasoning:** Per brief §3.1, MAD now carries two concern-domains whose coherence has drifted apart. The engine-kernel surface governs *how* a deliberation is convened and recorded for any trigger — the mechanics a reader needs for *any* OI. The OI-004 surface is the set of sections introduced in v3 and v4 that articulate *what closure looks like for one particular Open Issue* — the four sections totalling 1,463 words. A reader convening an OI-015 or OI-018 deliberation needs the first surface in full and the second as reference; a reader closing OI-004 needs the second in full and the first as reference. The file's current merger is a historical artefact of OI-004 being the seed case, not a deliberate decision that engine-kernel and OI-004-closure live together.

Three pieces of evidence the content seam is real, not manufactured by budget pressure: (i) the OI-004 sections all carry "for OI-004" in their heading per brief §3.1 — no other MAD section is OI-scoped this way, and the naming is the spec telling us these sections are already logically distinct; (ii) validator checks 16-19 and Tier 2 Q8 per brief §5 / Q5 are the enforcement surface specifically for this material and would port cleanly to the new file as the authoritative cite-target; (iii) the v4 additions accumulated 1,314 words *within one engine-v cycle* — a coherent body of new work that arrived together and now reads as a unit. The content grew its own skin; A.2 gives that skin a body.

The brief's §2 seam — "the four v4-added OI-004 sections" — is the one I endorse. I would not extend the split to §Open Extensions or §Heterogeneous-Participant Recording Schema; those are engine-kernel concerns even when bulky.

## Q4. If the chosen shape splits or relocates, how does it engage WX-22-1 laundering-as-codification?
