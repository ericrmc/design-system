## Q2

Among the four candidate P4 cases, only **S017 H4 layered model** clearly meets the strict threshold.

- **S017 H4 layered model: qualifies.** This is the strongest case because the contradiction is at the **frame level**, not merely at the detail level. The Claude perspectives occupied H1/H2/H3; the Outsider rejected that option-set and proposed H4 as a fourth hypothesis. The synthesis records that explicitly: the Outsider “explicitly rejects both H1 and H2 and proposes a fourth hypothesis” and describes H4 as “rejecting H1/H2/H3’s either/or framing” [provenance/017-oi017-reframing-deliberation/01-deliberation.md, §2.6]. The synthesis then recommends “Adopt H4” and the adopted shape is explicitly Outsider-originated [provenance/017-oi017-reframing-deliberation/01-deliberation.md, §6]. That is not supplementation. The Outsider contradicted the Claude-framed decision space, and the synthesis adopted the contradiction.

- **S014 three-cell protocol: borderline but does not cleanly qualify.** The synthesis plainly says the three-cell protocol was Outsider-originated and adopted, and that “no Claude perspective produced the three-cell framing at this level” [provenance/014-oi016-resolution/01-deliberation.md, Q3; §Q3 position]. But the same synthesis also says Operationalist and Skeptic already favored hand-off-containing multi-session shapes, and the adopted mechanism integrates Operationalist and Skeptic logic into the final shape [provenance/014-oi016-resolution/01-deliberation.md, Q3]. That is a strong non-Claude contribution, but not clearly “contradicted Claude consensus and prevailed.” There was no unified Claude consensus for the Outsider to contradict; there was a Claude split. I would not use S014 as the load-bearing P4 citation if a cleaner case exists.

- **S021 two-branch criterion-4 structure: does not qualify.** The synthesis itself is explicit that this is “frame-completion at the structural level” and “weaker than Session 017 H4” [provenance/021-oi004-criterion4-articulation/01-deliberation.md, §3.5; §7 Honest notes]. It also states that the adopted R1 text combines a 4-of-4 convergent core with the Outsider’s bifurcation as the spec-text frame [provenance/021-oi004-criterion4-articulation/01-deliberation.md, §4 R1]. That means the Outsider shaped organisation and articulation, but did not prevail over a Claude consensus in the required contradictory sense.

- **S036 workspace-identity-file class: does not qualify.** The synthesis credits the Outsider with the “workspace-identity-file” distinction and says it was adopted [provenance/036-session-assessment/01-deliberation.md, §5; §7]. But the same synthesis also shows that the Outsider’s own primary dispatch position was partly rejected in favour of a hybrid; the final direction is “the Synthesiser’s layered hybrid, informed by Outsider’s category-error critique” [provenance/036-session-assessment/01-deliberation.md, §3c]. So the correct P4 answer is: **S017 qualifies; S014 is arguable but weaker; S021 and S036 do not.**

That would collapse “successor adjudication” into “the gate opens by walking through it.” Because Session 017 already supplies the needed case, this recursion is acceptable here.

On Session 021’s R4 three grounds:

- **Cross-family threshold:** mostly holds up. Requiring a successor-session, non-Claude deliberation was a good anti-laundering defence.
- **Convergence-by-mechanism:** only partially holds up. It justified one deferral, not twenty. Repeated mechanism-presence did not by itself force closure discipline [provenance/021-oi004-criterion4-articulation/01-deliberation.md, §7 Honest notes].
- **Asymmetry argument:** it was correct in Session 021 against same-session closure. It is no longer a reason to keep OI-004 open in Session 041 once the record supports condition (iii).

My GPT-family-specific honesty point is this: this workspace has operationally exercised **one** non-Claude lineage repeatedly, not “cross-family pluralism” in the broad sense. That narrower fact is enough to close OI-004 **as currently specified**. It is not enough to claim the methodology has solved independence in the general case. The limitation note remains load-bearing.
